Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Passion

DulyDecors was founded in 2012 by Arvind Kumar, a civil engineer with a vision to bring international-quality villa construction to the Indian homeowner. What began as a small construction firm in Hyderabad has grown into a pan-India brand trusted by over 250 families.

Our journey has been one of relentless improvement. From adopting cutting-edge construction technology to hand-picking the finest craftsmen, every decision we make is guided by one question: "What would make our client prouder of this home?"

Today, DulyDecors offers the complete spectrum of home-building services — from architectural design and structural construction to luxury interiors and full turnkey solutions — all under one trusted roof.

Our Direction

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and admired villa construction company — one that every homeowner thinks of first when they dream of building their perfect home. We envision a future where quality construction is accessible, transparent, and exceptional across every city we serve.

Our Mission

To deliver world-class construction and interior design services with complete transparency, on-time delivery, and uncompromising quality — while building lasting relationships with every client, contractor, and community we serve. Our mission is to turn every client's vision into a home they'll cherish forever.

Our Journey

A Decade of Building Dreams

1987

Founded in Chennai

Started with a 1-member and our first residential project in Maduravoyal. The foundation of a legacy.

2000

Launched Interior Design Division

Expanded into luxury interior design, offering clients a seamless construction-to-interior experience.

2021

Crossed 500+ Interiors Projects

A major milestone. Our team grew to 30+ professionals and we expanded operations to Chennai and Bengaluru.

2021

Introduced Villa Projects

Launched our popular end-to-end turnkey offering, taking clients from raw land to a furnished, move-in-ready home.

2024

Pan-India Operations

Now serving 15+ cities across India with 45+ team members and 250+ completed projects, and counting.
