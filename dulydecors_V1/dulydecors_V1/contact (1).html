Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a villa?
Typically, a 3–4 BHK villa of 3,000–4,500 sq.ft takes 12–18 months from foundation to handover. Timeline depends on the design complexity, site conditions, and the scope of interiors. We provide a detailed project schedule at the planning stage.
What is the cost of constructing a villa?
Construction costs vary based on design complexity, materials chosen, and location. Our standard construction starts from ₹1,800/sq.ft for basic finishes and ₹2,800+/sq.ft for premium finishes. We provide a detailed, itemised quotation with no hidden charges after the initial consultation.
Do you provide a structural warranty?
Yes. We provide a 10-year structural warranty on all our villa construction projects, covering the structural integrity of the building. Additionally, we offer a 1-year defect liability period for finishing works.
Can I see sample work before committing?
Absolutely. We encourage site visits to completed projects (with client permission). You can also explore our extensive portfolio gallery and request detailed case studies for projects similar to yours.
What is a turnkey project and how does it work?
A turnkey project means we handle everything — from architectural design, municipal approvals, and structural construction to interior furnishing and landscaping. You simply provide the plot and your requirements, and we deliver a fully move-in-ready home on the agreed date.
Do you work outside Chennai?
Yes! We currently operate across Chennai and nearby areas including Maduravoyal, Porur, Mogappair, Poonamallee, Mangadu, Kundrathur, Manimangalam, Avadi and surrounding locations. Contact us to check service availability in your area.
